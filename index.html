Hike the Bay Area!

Get outside...

The Bay Area and surrounding lands provide awesome, breathtaking hikes. Whether you are a beginner or an old pro, there a hike for you.

New to the Bay Area?

A lot of visitors to the Bay Area are here to see the Golden Gate bridge, Alcatraz, and Lombard Street- but there are a lot of amazing natural sites to see outside the hub of the city!

What to bring:

Here is a list of things I always bring along on my hikes. You can make adjustments depending on the length of the hike and the weather.

  • water bottle
  • snacks (possibly a full meal)
  • layers (sweater, zip up)
  • sunscreen
  • mini first aid kit
  • cell phone
  • hat
  • sunglasses
  • rain jacket
  • appropriate footwear
  • hiking poles
